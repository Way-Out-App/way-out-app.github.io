You can send anonymous comments using an email address that does not reveal your identity, and does not include your name. You can unsubscribe at any time from our mailing list announcing the launch of new cities by clicking on the "Unsubscribe" link in emails that you may receive.

7. DISCLOSURE TO THIRD PARTY

We do not disclose information to third parties without your explicit consent except in the following cases:

  • In the event of a potential business transaction, you agree that Wayout may disclose anonymous and aggregated information to another company to allow parties to determine whether to continue the transaction.
  • In the event that the business transaction concludes successfully, you agree that any information may be transmitted to the Company in part or in full ownership of Wayout in order to continue its business. A "business transaction" includes, but is not limited to, the reorganization, merger, purchase, sale or other acquisition or disposal of a company or any part of its assets.
  • In the event that we are required to comply with laws and regulations and legal requests and orders or if permitted by law (ie, for the protection and defense of our rights, situation which threatens life, health or safety, etc.).

We also disclose anonymous and aggregated information to third parties for research, product development or other business purposes. For example, we can share anonymous and aggregated information with transit agencies to help them analyze and improve their services.

8. SECURITY MEASURES

We are committed to securing your information and we take reasonable physical, organizational, and technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

These measures include, but are not limited to:

  • Storage on secure servers;
  • Encription of transmitted data;
  • Limited access to employees and contractors based on the "need to know".

While we take every measure possible to protect your information, no security system can be completely safe from any security breaches, misdeeds or potential failures.

9. CONSERVATION PERIOD

We will retain your information for as long as you use Wayout and thereafter for a reasonable time.

10. RIGHT OF ACCESS AND DELETION

We store your information on servers located in Canada.

If you wish to remove or access any information collected by Wayout stored on our servers, please contact us at info@wayoutapp.com. Upon receipt of your request, we will use reasonable efforts to provide or remove such information.

11. CHILDREN

We do not knowingly collect, use or disclose personal information of children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under the age of 13 without prior parental consent, we will take steps to remove this information within a reasonable time. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove this information within a reasonable amount of time.

12. UPDATE

We reserve the right to amend or change this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. In the event that substantial changes are made to this privacy policy, we will alert you of these changes through our social networks 30 days before they come into effect. By using Wayout, you agree to be bound by the version of the privacy policy posted on the date you used the application. If you do not accept any amendments or changes to the privacy policy, you may not access, use or continue to use Wayout. By continuing to access and use Wayout, you signify your acceptance of the said amendments and modifications. In any case, we recommend that you periodically visit this web page to be informed of any changes in this privacy policy.

When we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will review the "Last Updated" date at the end of this Privacy Policy.

13. CONTACT INFORMATION

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at info@wayoutapp.com and we will review and respond to all your communications in a timely manner.
