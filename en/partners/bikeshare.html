Bikeshare & Scooters

Get More People
on Two Wheels

We help urbanites discover the convenience of point-to-point services, and the pleasure of running errands or sightseeing on their own schedules. By integrating your bikeshare or scooter system into our app, Transit makes it easy for riders to find a ride, buy passes, and start trips with their phones.

Integrated Mobility for Your Riders

Transit is a one-stop shop: users can see when the next bus is coming, book carshare, request a ridehail trip or buy a bikeshare pass all in the same place.

Attract Tourists

Millions of people use Transit across the globe. We make it easier for tourists to try bikeshare or scooters when they come to town — without downloading a new app.

Reach New Clients

With the constant visibility of your bikeshare or scooter system on our app, locals already using Transit will be more likely to take that first, magical trip.

Increase Visibility

Our app features your system and sponsors’ branding, letting users know exactly who’s helping them to get around.

An Instant Success

Our Divvy partnership launched in May 2016. Now, users in Chicago buy thousands of passes and unlock tens of thousands of bikes through our app each month.
